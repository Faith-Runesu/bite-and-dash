About Us

Bite & Dash is a bakery brand born out of a love for the little things that bring big joy. We specialize in small, dainty treats that are easy to grab, gift, and enjoy on the go. From moist mini cakes to decadent slice surprises, every item is crafted to pack a punch of flavor in just the right portion. Whether you're celebrating a moment or treating yourself on a whim, Bite & Dash delivers small bakes with big personality.
